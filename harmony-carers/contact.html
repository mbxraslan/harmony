Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I register for NDIS services?
To register for NDIS services, you'll need to contact the National Disability Insurance Agency (NDIA) and complete an Access Request Form. Our team can guide you through this process and provide support during your application.
What areas do you service?
We currently provide services throughout the Sydney metropolitan area, with a focus on the Western and Southern suburbs. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, please contact us directly.
How soon can I start receiving care?
After your initial consultation, we can typically arrange for care to begin within 48-72 hours. For urgent cases, we may be able to expedite this process. Please contact us directly if you require immediate assistance.
Do you offer emergency services?
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency support for our registered clients. Our after-hours team is available to assist with urgent care needs. Please use our emergency contact number for immediate assistance.
